## Summary of Findings

A single `whoami` command was executed on agent **dc** (domain controller) by **CONDEF\Administrator** at 00:46 UTC on 2026-06-29, spawned from an interactive `cmd.exe` shell. While `whoami` is a built-in command, its execution on a domain controller under the domain Administrator account during off-hours is a high-confidence discovery indicator. Adversaries routinely run `whoami` immediately after establishing a foothold or elevating privileges to confirm their effective identity prior to pivoting. This warrants immediate triage to determine whether the Administrator session was authorized.
